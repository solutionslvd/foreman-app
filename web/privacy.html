Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: March 6, 2025  |  Effective: March 6, 2025

← Back to Home

1. Introduction

Welcome to Foreman ("we," "our," or "us"). Foreman is a construction business management platform designed for contractors, tradespeople, and construction companies. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application and services. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services.

Foreman is operated from Alberta, Canada, and we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, company name, phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash).

Business Data: Information you enter into the app including projects, invoices, expenses, payroll records, employee information, and financial data. This data belongs to you.

Documents & Files: Files you upload including blueprints, contracts, permits, photos, and other documents. These are stored securely and associated with your account.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use the app, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

Communications: If you contact us for support, we may retain records of that correspondence.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Foreman platform
  • Process your transactions and manage your account
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Power the AI assistant features (your data is sent to AI providers to generate responses)
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your business data for advertising purposes.

4. AI Features & Third-Party AI Providers

Foreman includes an AI assistant powered by third-party AI services (OpenAI and/or Anthropic). When you use the AI chat feature:

  • Your messages and relevant app data (projects, invoices, etc.) are sent to the AI provider to generate responses
  • AI providers may retain data according to their own privacy policies
  • We recommend not sharing sensitive personal information (SIN numbers, banking credentials) in AI chat
  • You can disable AI features in your account settings

AI providers we use: OpenAI (privacy policy at openai.com/privacy) and Anthropic (privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy).

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • JWT-based authentication tokens
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your data is stored in Canada and/or the United States. By using Foreman, you consent to this transfer.

6. Client-Facing Features

Foreman allows you to share project progress links with your clients. When you use this feature:

  • Only information you explicitly mark as "client visible" is shared
  • Client links are accessible to anyone with the link URL
  • You are responsible for managing what information is shared with clients
  • You can revoke client access by deleting the project link

Your clients' contact information (name, email, phone) is stored as part of your project data and is subject to this Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your personal information will be deleted within 30 days
  • Business records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Canadian tax law
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights (PIPEDA / PIPA)

Under Canadian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent to our use of your data (may affect service availability)
  • Complaint: File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise these rights, contact us at the information below.

9. Cookies & Tracking

Foreman uses minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Authentication tokens: Stored in browser localStorage to keep you logged in
  • App data: Your business data is cached in localStorage for offline functionality
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use third-party advertising trackers

You can clear your browser's localStorage at any time, which will log you out and clear cached data.

10. Children's Privacy

Foreman is a business application intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page with an updated date
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a notice in the app

Your continued use of Foreman after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
